"A Clean Slate for Every Veteran"

The Clean Slate Houston Foundation exists to ensure that every veteran in the greater Houston area has access to stable housing, meaningful employment, and the support systems needed to rebuild and thrive — regardless of their past circumstances.

What We Do

Five Pillars of Impact

The Foundation's work spans five interconnected program areas — each rooted in D&D's 20+ years of experience serving the Houston veteran community.

🏠

Adaptive Housing Services

Providing accessible, modified housing solutions for veterans with disabilities and physical limitations — connecting individuals to home modification programs, accessible rentals, and transitional housing resources throughout Houston.

💼

Employment & Workforce Development

Job readiness training, apprenticeship program navigation, employer partnerships, and career coaching to help veterans achieve lasting economic self-sufficiency and dignified employment.

🤝

Homeless Veteran Outreach

Street-level outreach, coordinated entry support, and case management for homeless and at-risk veterans — connecting individuals to shelter, healthcare, benefits, and permanent housing pathways.

👨‍👩‍👧

Veteran Family Support

Wraparound services for veteran families — connecting spouses, children, and dependents to healthcare navigation, mental health resources, and community support throughout the greater Houston area.

🌟

Benefits Navigation & Advocacy

Helping veterans access the VA benefits, disability claims, housing vouchers, and community resources they have earned — cutting through bureaucratic barriers with knowledgeable, veteran-centered advocacy.

3

File Form 424 — TX SOS Name Change

Amendment to Certificate of Formation filed with the Texas Secretary of State — updating the organization name to Clean Slate Houston Foundation and the purpose clause to reflect the veteran-focused mission. Fee: $25. Processing: 3–5 business days.

4

IRS Name Change Notification

Notify the IRS of the name change by letter to the Exempt Organizations unit, attaching the amended Certificate of Formation. No new Form 1023 required — the existing determination letter carries forward.

5

File Form 8822-B — Update IRS Records

Update the responsible party, mailing address, and organization name in IRS records. Free to file. Processing: 4–6 weeks.

6

Update Bank & Vendor Accounts

Update financial accounts, vendor registrations, and any state registrations to reflect the new name. Present amended Certificate of Formation as proof of name change.
