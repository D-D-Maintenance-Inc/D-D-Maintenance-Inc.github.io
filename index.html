Who We Are

A Business Built on Serving Veterans

D&D Maintenance Clean Slate Houston provides veterans a real path to stability, employment, and dignity. We believe those who served our country deserve every opportunity to thrive.

Veteran Employment First We hire, train, and place veterans and non-veterans into meaningful jobs across the Houston metro area.
Clean Slate Houston Our staffing arm helps individuals with backgrounds gain full-time employment, benefits, and a fresh start.
